About Me

So, who am I?

I'm Kaelyn, a 21 year old developer based in Brighton, United Kingdom. I never went to college or university for any sort of development; I've always preferred learning on my own.

I took my first dive into this world when I was approximately 12, learning ROBLOX's adaptation of Lua (Luau) for various small projects. I met a lot of different people through ROBLOX, some people I'm still inseprible from to this day.

One of the reasons I took the dive into development was because I always found it fascinating, mainly because I was never a creative person. I could never draw, I could never write, I could never do anything that required creativity. I always found myself to be a logical person, and I found that development was a great way to express that.

I wouldn't go so far as to call myself a "professional", far from it. I'm still learning, but I'm not the sort of person that learns by reading tutorials, watching videos or even participating in online courses. I learn by getting stuck in and doing the task. I've always found it far more useful to make mistakes and have errors in your code, because then you learn how to write code in that specific language as well as valuable debugging skills.
